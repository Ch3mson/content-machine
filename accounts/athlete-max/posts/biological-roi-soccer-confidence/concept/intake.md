## Target Avatar
Competitive soccer players (any position) who want to feel more confident on the pitch

## Pain Point
Confidence disappears in key moments — penalties, one-on-ones, first touch under pressure, playing in front of scouts or parents. The player knows they have the skill in training but freezes or plays small when it matters.

## Desired Transformation
Confidence that survives pressure — not "fake it till you make it" motivation, but a biological system that keeps you sharp when the stakes are high.

## Emotional Lever
The gap between training confidence and game-day confidence. The frustration of knowing you're good enough but not feeling it when it counts.

## Product Constraints
w(inner) must be extremely camouflaged, almost invisible

## What To Avoid
Motivational language, bro-science, unsourced claims, generic "believe in yourself" advice, position-specific jargon that excludes half the audience
