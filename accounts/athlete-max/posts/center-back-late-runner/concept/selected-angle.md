# Selected Angle

## Angle

The mistake is not late reaction speed. It is late information.

## Reader

A soccer center back who still feels sharp enough to run, but starts losing
runners behind the shoulder late in the match.

## Private Gap

They watched the passer and still missed the runner. That makes the mistake feel
like a reaction problem, when the more useful fix is scanning earlier.

## Mechanism

Fatigue and game pressure can narrow attention toward the most obvious cue: the
ball. A center back needs to update ball, runner, and space before the pass, not
after the pass exposes the run.

## Fix Direction

Use a two-second scan loop after the main session:

1. Scan ball.
2. Scan runner.
3. Scan space behind the line.
4. Call the runner before turning back to the ball.
