# Men's Fitness And Athlete Mental Performance Source Bank

This source bank is for the men's fitness pilot account, now grounded in w(inner): a mental performance app for athletes. This account should prioritize athletes, sport performance, confidence, pressure, visualization, recovery from mistakes, and competition preparation over generic bodybuilding or nutrition content.

## Source Policy

- Use sources for sport psychology, visualization, performance anxiety, recovery, sleep, injury return, or measurable outcome claims.
- Use product claims only when supported by `../../product/app-brief.md` or `../../product/claim-bank.md`.
- Frame unsupported ideas as opinion, coaching observation, or draft claims.
- Avoid clinical mental health advice unless a qualified source and careful wording support it.
- Avoid nutrition, hypertrophy, supplement, or medical claims unless the account intentionally expands there later.

## Source Categories To Prefer

- Peer-reviewed sport psychology research.
- Official or university sport psychology resources.
- Sports medicine sources for injury-return confidence.
- Sleep research or official sleep guidance for night-before claims.
- Credible coach, athlete, or performance practitioner interviews for voice and examples.
- Product screenshots, demos, or user proof when making product-specific claims.

## Source Categories To Avoid

- Generic motivational posts.
- Unsourced "mindset hack" threads.
- Supplement or biohacking claims.
- Clinical mental health content repackaged as performance advice.
- Any source that promises guaranteed confidence, selection, winning, or recovery.
