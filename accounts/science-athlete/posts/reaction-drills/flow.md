- **Copy:**
  - Header: `the gap:`
  - Title: `cone drills train the wrong system`
  - Body: `Cones on the ground force your gaze down. You track each cone with center vision. You get better at looking. You don't get better at seeing. The rapid direction changes feel hard. They are also the least game-specific part.`
