# Intake: Winger Training Transfer

## Account
- science-athlete

## Avatar
Soccer winger (youth, collegiate, serious amateur)

## Pain Point
Dominates in team training and 1v1s — beats players, looks fluid, feels confident — but shrinks in real games. Skills do not transfer to match performance.

## Desired Transformation
Translate practice performance into game performance effortlessly. Show up in matches the way they show up in training.

## Likely Emotional Lever
The identity gap between "I know I can do this" and "Why do I disappear when it counts?" Frustration, self-doubt, fear of not belonging at their level.

## What The Post Should Avoid
Generic "just be confident" advice, blaming mentality, suggesting the athlete isn't trying hard enough.

## Product Relevance
w(inner) visualization / mental reps can bridge game-picture confidence after the physical mechanism is explained.

## Claim Constraints
No performance guarantees. Product mention only after physical/training fix is established.

## User Note
Research should focus on root pain, solutions that failed, and ideas that are different from all failed options.
