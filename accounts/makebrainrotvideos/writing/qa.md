# Quality Checks

Final copy checks before approving any draft for rendering. Run after all three
refinement personas have passed.

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] **Hook stops the scroll.** Would the Broke Scroller stop for this in under
  2 seconds? If not, rewrite the hook before checking anything else.
- [ ] **Every slide has one idea.** No slide contains two concepts, a comma
  separating ideas, or more than one sentence.
- [ ] **5-7 slides.** Post falls within the optimal range. Exceptions need
  justification.
- [ ] **Fragment-style copy.** No full sentences on slides. Phrases and fragments
  only.
- [ ] **Specificity check.** At least one specific number, tool name, or
  verifiable claim in the post.
- [ ] **No banned phrases.** Cross-check against `banned-patterns.md`.
- [ ] **Product placement correct.** MakeBrainrotVideos.com appears on last 1-2
  slides only, or not at all. Never on slide 1.
- [ ] **Caption has the CTA.** Engagement CTA lives in the caption, not on a
  slide. ("Save for later," "Comment X," "Follow for more.")
- [ ] **Income claims qualified.** Any dollar amount is either factual, qualified
  ("top creators," "results vary"), or backed by proof (screenshot).
- [ ] **Three personas passed.** Broke Scroller, Account-Native Scroller, and
  Bullshit Detector all cleared.
- [ ] **Stop-slop check.** No AI-sounding filler, formulaic structures, or
  generic wellness language. See `../../references/skills/stop-slop/SKILL.md`.

## Scoring

Score each dimension 1-10 before approving.

| Dimension | What it measures | Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Recognition | Would the target audience instantly know this is for them? | 7+ |
| Curiosity | Does slide 1 create a need to see slide 2? | 8+ |
| Specificity | Are claims, tools, and numbers concrete? | 7+ |
| Rhythm | Does the copy flow with short, punchy, swipeable momentum? | 8+ |
| Trust | Would the Bullshit Detector let this pass? | 7+ |
| Fit | Does it feel native to @wealth_builders_x-style accounts? | 7+ |
| Persona fit | Did all three personas pass without blocking failures? | Pass/Fail |

**Minimum to approve**: All dimensions 7+, Persona fit Pass, total 42+/60.
