## Candidate Samples

### Candidate A: Tool-Reveal Flex (First-Person)

**Angle**: First-person income flex + tool reveal for MakeBrainrotVideos.com.
**Hook type**: Number + method.
**Why it should work**: Combines the income-claim hook pattern with a tool reveal.
Progressive disclosure builds to the product as the payoff.

| Slide | Copy |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | I made 200 AI videos in 10 minutes |
| 2 | Step 1: Pick a viral prompt |
| 3 | Step 2: Hit generate |
| 4 | Step 3: AI makes 10+ variations |
| 5 | Step 4: Post the best ones |
| 6 | The tool: MakeBrainrotVideos.com |

**Caption**: Save this for later. Link in bio.
**Hashtags**: #aitools #makemoneyonline #ai #tiktok #viral #contentcreator

**Persona check**:
- Broke Scroller: "200 videos in 10 minutes" is specific and disbelief-inducing. Pass.
- Account-Native Scroller: Fragment style, one idea per slide, 6 slides. Pass.
- Bullshit Detector: Claim is factually accurate (mass generation is the product's
  core feature). Pass.

### Candidate B: Tutorial How-To (Educational)

**Angle**: Step-by-step tutorial for making money with AI videos.
**Hook type**: Specific income + method.
**Why it should work**: Tutorial format drives saves. Specific income claim stops
the scroll. Steps build progressive disclosure.

| Slide | Copy |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | How people make $3K/month with AI videos |
| 2 | Step 1: Pick a trending niche |
| 3 | Step 2: Mass generate videos with AI |
| 4 | Step 3: Post 5-10x per day across accounts |
| 5 | Step 4: Monetize with affiliate links |
| 6 | The volume is the strategy |
| 7 | Tool I use: link in bio |

**Caption**: Comment "AI" and I'll send you the method.
**Hashtags**: #makemoneyonline #aivideos #sidehustle #contentcreator #rich #fyp

**Persona check**:
- Broke Scroller: "$3K/month" is specific. "How people make" is third-person
  (slightly weaker than first-person). Borderline pass.
- Account-Native Scroller: 7 slides, fragment style, progressive disclosure. Pass.
- Bullshit Detector: "$3K/month" is qualified with "people" not "you will." Volume
  strategy is legitimate. Pass.

### Candidate C: Curiosity Gap (Trend-Jack)

**Angle**: "Stop doing X, do Y instead" format.
**Hook type**: Curiosity gap + command.
**Why it should work**: Trend format with built-in engagement. Challenges existing
behavior, which creates tension.

| Slide | Copy |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Stop making one video at a time |
| 2 | Creators who go viral mass produce |
| 3 | They test 50 ideas per week |
| 4 | Then double down on winners |
| 5 | One tool does this automatically |
| 6 | MakeBrainrotVideos.com |

**Caption**: This changed everything. Save for later.
**Hashtags**: #aitools #ai #viral #contentcreator #makemoneyonline #fyp

**Persona check**:
- Broke Scroller: "Stop making one video at a time" challenges behavior. Creates
  curiosity. Pass.
- Account-Native Scroller: 6 slides, command hook, progressive disclosure to
  product reveal. Pass.
- Bullshit Detector: "Mass produce" and "test 50 ideas" are legitimate strategies.
  "Automatically" is slightly strong but accurate for the tool. Pass.
