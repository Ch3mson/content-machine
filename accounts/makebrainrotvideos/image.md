This file controls photo subject direction, visual treatment, and image
selection criteria. Layout, typography, and frame placement belong in
`design.md`.

## Photo Subject Direction

### Primary image categories

| Category | Use for | Example subjects |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Money / wealth | Hook slides, flex posts | Cash on table, stacked bills, luxury watches, bank app screenshots |
| Lifestyle / luxury | Hook slides, body slides | Sports cars (Lamborghini, Ferrari), Dubai skyline, penthouse views, private jets |
| Tech / workspace | Tutorial steps, tool reveals | MacBook setup, multi-monitor desk, phone showing analytics, ring light setup |
| Platform screenshots | Tool steps, proof slides | TikTok analytics, YouTube dashboard, MakeBrainrotVideos.com UI, earnings reports |
| City / ambition | Hook slides, aspirational | Night skylines, skyscraper views, rooftop shots, city streets at night |

### Secondary (use sparingly)

| Category | Use for | Example subjects |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Faceless creator setup | Tutorial posts | Hands on keyboard, phone recording, editing timeline |
| Before/after | Transformation posts | Old setup vs new setup, $0 vs $X earnings side-by-side |

### Do not use

- Generic stock photos (smiling businesspeople, handshake photos).
- Clip art, illustrations, or cartoon graphics (exception: rare infographic-style posts).
- Bright, pastel, or "wellness" aesthetics.
- Photos with visible watermarks.
- Images of specific identifiable people without rights.
- Anything that looks like it belongs on a corporate website.

## Visual Treatment

### Standard treatment (most slides)

1. **Dark overlay**: 60-80% opacity black layer over the photo. Adjust per slide
   type (see `design.md` for specific values).
2. **Contrast boost**: +10-15% contrast increase on the source photo before
   overlay. Makes details pop through the dark layer.
3. **Saturation**: Slight desaturation (-10-20%). Keeps the mood dark and serious.
   Do not go full B&W — this account is not athlete-stories.
4. **Color temperature**: Neutral to slightly cool (blue tint). Matches the
   tech/money aesthetic. No warm or sepia tones.

### Product slide treatment

- Always use a sourced background image (Pinterest/aesthetic photo) with a dark overlay, matching the rest of the post.
- Do not use a solid dark background for the CTA slide.

### Processing pipeline order

1. Source photo (center crop to 1080x1920).
2. Contrast boost (+10-15%).
3. Slight desaturation (-10-20%).
4. Cool color shift (optional, subtle).
5. Dark overlay at specified opacity.
6. Text rendering (see `design.md`).
7. Export PNG.

## Photo Selection Criteria

Before using a photo, check:

- [ ] High resolution (minimum 1080px wide, ideally larger for crop flexibility).
- [ ] Subject matches the slide content (money photo for money slide, tech for
  tool slide, etc.).
- [ ] No competing text or logos in the source photo.
- [ ] Dark-overlay-friendly: photo should still be recognizable at 60-80% overlay.
  Very dark photos will look like a black slide.
- [ ] Aspirational but believable. Lamborghini is fine. Private island with
  helicopter is too much for this audience.
- [ ] Consistent with other slides in the same post. Do not mix luxury cars with
  coffee shop photos in one post.

## Image Sourcing Notes

- Pinterest: search "luxury lifestyle dark", "money aesthetic", "tech setup
  dark", "dubai night skyline", "cash on table aesthetic".
- Unsplash/Pexels: search similar terms. Prefer photos with dark backgrounds
  or nighttime shots (less overlay needed).
- Screenshots: capture from the actual MakeBrainrotVideos.com dashboard, TikTok
  analytics, or YouTube Studio for tutorial slides.
- AI-generated: acceptable for lifestyle/city imagery if quality is high enough.
  Not acceptable for product screenshots or earnings proof.
