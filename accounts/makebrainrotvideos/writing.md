## Core Voice Snapshot

- **Tone**: Direct, confident, slightly cocky. Talk like someone who already
  made the money and is casually telling you how.
- **Person**: Mix of first-person flex ("I made 200 AI videos in 10 minutes")
  and second-person tutorial ("Step 1: Pick a viral prompt").
- **Rhythm**: 3-8 words per slide. Fragments, not sentences. Fast, punchy,
  every slide earns the next swipe.
- **Authority**: "I did this" or "here's how." The account IS the expert.
  Never cite studies or external authorities.
- **Slang**: Light internet/hustle slang OK ("bread," "no cap"). Never forced.
- **Product voice**: MakeBrainrotVideos.com is the tool behind the results.
  Mentioned on last 1-2 slides as the reveal, never as the pitch.

## Locked Rules

These rules are non-negotiable once this file is locked. Currently candidates
pending quality gate.

1. **Every hook needs a specific number or command.** No vague "make money"
   openers.
2. **One idea per slide.** If there is a comma separating ideas, split the
   slide.
3. **5-7 slides per post.** Hard limits: minimum 5, maximum 8.
4. **Fragments only on slides.** No full sentences. No paragraphs.
5. **Caption is the CTA.** Engagement instructions go in the caption, not on
   slides.
6. **Product on last 1-2 slides only.** Never open with the product.
7. **No unqualified income guarantees.** Qualify with "top creators," "results
   vary," or show proof.
8. **No banned phrases.** See `writing/banned-patterns.md`.
9. **All three personas must pass.** No draft skips the refinement protocol.

## Draft/Candidate Rules

These are under consideration and may be promoted or cut after quality gate.

- Tutorial posts should use numbered steps (Step 1, Step 2, etc.).
- Trend-jacking hooks should be used at least 1 in 4 posts.
- "Save for later" and "Comment X" CTAs should alternate.
- Flex posts and tutorial posts should alternate roughly 1:1.
- **Caption format:** User strongly prefers long-form, "mini-blog" style captions that expand on the slides rather than simple one-liners.
