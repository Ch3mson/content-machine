# Source Bank: Athlete Stories

## Source Policy

- Use sources for high-risk factual claims.
- Use sources for product claims unless the claim already appears in `../../product/app-brief.md` or `../../product/claim-bank.md`.
- Frame unsupported ideas as opinion, observation, or draft claims.
- Do not turn a source into a stronger claim than it supports.

## Niche Research Map

| Content pillar | Claim risk | Source type needed | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Athlete childhood / origin | Low | Biography, documentary, verified interview | Safe if widely reported |
| Training routines / grind | Medium | Primary interview, autobiography | Avoid unsourced workout claims |
| Mental visualization / peak moment | Medium | Athlete interview, documentary quote | Flag if paraphrased heavily |
| Career outcomes / legacy | Low | Public record, stats | Safe |
| Product integration | High | Product brief / claim bank only | No invented claims |

## Approved Sources

| Source | Topic | Link or file | Useful claim | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Athlete autobiographies | Origin / grind | — | Direct quotes and verified facts | Preferred for origin stories |
| Documentary interviews | Visualization / mindset | — | Athlete describing mental habits | Preferred for peak slides |
| Verified sports journalism | Career facts | — | Stats, draft position, championships | Safe for transformation slides |

## Source Categories To Prefer

- Autobiographies and memoirs
- Documentary interviews (e.g., The Last Dance)
- Verified long-form sports journalism

## Source Categories To Avoid

- Unverified social media rumors
- Aggregator sites without primary attribution
- Fan forums and Wikipedia-only claims

## Recurring Concepts

| Concept | Safe framing | Risky framing | Source needed? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Visualization habit | "He visualized the court before stepping on it" | "He used a structured mental training system" | Medium — prefer primary source |
| Struggle / poverty | "He slept on couches" | "He was homeless" | Low if widely reported |
| Product use | "I use w(inner) to build a visualization track" | "w(inner) made him great" | Must come from claim bank |

## Words Or Claims To Avoid

- "Guaranteed"
- "Cure"
- "Therapy"
- "Meditation app"
- Clinical anxiety or mental health diagnosis language
- Scholarship/contract/victory promises
