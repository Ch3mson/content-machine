# Athlete Stories Presets

Use this file to switch between established slideshow styles inside the `athlete-stories` account.

## Preset Index

| Preset | Use for | Opening behavior | Middle arc | Peak | Product bridge | Closing quote type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| LeBron | Origin-story rise, inevitability, grounded hardship | Facts only | Work, study, steady build | Visualization as continuation of work | Native, brief, low-friction | Identity / destiny |
| Michael Jordan | Rivalry, humiliation, pressure, invisible edge | Second-person challenge | Defeat, pressure, body not enough | Visualization as secret edge | Must echo Slide 5 directly | Belief / expectation |

## 1) LeBron Preset

### Summary

- Best for athlete stories that start low and build into greatness through steady proof.
- Detached editorial narrator.
- Slide 1 stays factual and staccato.
- Product mention stays subtle.

### Structure

1. The Beginning — factual origin pain
2. The Grind — early work and belief
3. The Weight — study, pressure, repetition
4. The Peak — visualization reveal
5. The Transformation — public payoff
6. The Product — quiet mirror of the method
7. The Legend — quote about identity or destiny

### Model Post

- `accounts/athlete-stories/Lebron/flow.md`

## 2) Michael Jordan Preset

### Summary

- Best for athlete stories built around humiliation, elite pressure, revenge, and mental edge.
- Starts by challenging the audience directly.
- The physical solution fails first.
- Visualization becomes the hidden differentiator.
- Product slide must feel like a direct continuation of the remedy.

### Structure

1. The Beginning — second-person challenge
2. The Grind — name the force that beat him
3. The Weight — show the pressure lingering after the game
4. The Failure of Body — physical work still not enough
5. The Remedy of Mind — visualization becomes the edge
6. The Product — same method, applied through w(inner)
7. The Legend — quote about belief, expectation, or inner standard

### Model Post

- `accounts/athlete-stories/Michael Jordan/flow.md`

## Quick Selection Rule

- Choose **LeBron** when the story is about rise.
- Choose **Michael Jordan** when the story is about edge.

## Notes

- Both presets keep the same image system from `image.md`.
- Both presets must still follow `sources.md` for risky claims.
- The Michael Jordan preset is an intentional deviation from the original Slide 1 rule in `writing.md`.
