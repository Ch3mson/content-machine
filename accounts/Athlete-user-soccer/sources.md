# Sources

Use this file to track usable photo sources for the account.

## Source Platforms

- Pinterest (default)
- TikTok carousel downloads (via `tools/tiktok-photo-sourcer/`)
- Free stock sites with soccer/candid imagery
- Self-captured or contributed photos (preferred for long-term brand building)

## Search Direction

- Keywords: football training, soccer practice, youth soccer, academy training, grass pitch, dressing room, warm-up, gym work, recovery, night match, floodlights
- Avoid: press conference photos, award ceremony photos, celebrity/star player candids (unless specifically needed for a post about that player)
- Prefer: candid, teammate-POV, natural lighting, training-ground aesthetic

## Source Notes

- All sourced images must be croppable to 9:16 without losing the key subject.
- Prefer landscape-oriented source images (wider than 1080) that can be center-cropped to 9:16.
- Prioritize images with a clear, readable center third for text overlay.
- For product slides, a native phone-in-hand photo taken in a training/soccer context is the target.
