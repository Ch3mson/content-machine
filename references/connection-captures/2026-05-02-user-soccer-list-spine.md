## Connection

The closest user-type inspiration accounts use a native carousel structure built around lists: a hook opens a promise, then each slide delivers a numbered or implied item. This applies across avoid-lists, habit lists, identity-rule posts, invisible-competition contrasts, rapid transformation listicles, and failure-confession posts that turn into mistake lists. The athlete-user-soccer account should therefore default to hook + 3-5 modules, not a slow lesson arc.

## Transferable Principle

For user-type sports accounts, list structure carries native platform feel. The hook earns attention, but the incomplete list creates swipe momentum. Each item should be independently useful and visually readable as a slide.
