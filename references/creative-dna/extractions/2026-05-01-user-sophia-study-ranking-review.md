- Mechanism: It reduces uncertainty by converting subjective advice into a scored comparison.
- Why it works: The viewer trusts the process because the creator appears to have done the experimentation.
- Viewer belief being challenged: "All study advice is basically the same." 
- Viewer desire or insecurity being activated: wanting a clearer system and a higher-return method.
