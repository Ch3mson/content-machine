## Reusable Principle

The deeper creative mechanism that could transfer to another niche.

- Principle name: Hidden-noise diagnosis
- Principle summary: Reframe underperformance as the predictable result of constant micro-stimulation, then prescribe boring, low-friction contrast as the fix.
- Safe rewrite formula: If you feel stuck / flat / inconsistent, it may be because your baseline is too stimulated; reduce noise, tolerate boredom, then the desired behavior becomes easier.

## Psychological Mechanism

What makes the viewer care, pause, believe, or keep swiping?

- Why it works: The viewer gets relief from self-blame plus a concrete explanation for why willpower feels broken.
- Viewer belief being challenged: "I just need more discipline." 
- Viewer desire or insecurity being activated: Wanting focus, drive, and a sense that progress is still possible.
