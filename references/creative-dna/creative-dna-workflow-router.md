# Creative DNA Workflow Router

Use this router when turning a reusable viral principle into an account-specific
post idea.

The routing question is:

> Which creative mechanism fits the audience tension, and which workflow gate
> turns it into approved post copy without copying the source?

## Core Route

```text
Viral Reference Post
→ Creative DNA Extraction
→ Canonical Mechanism Family
→ Target Account Fit
→ Possible Post Concept
→ Post Concept Flow
→ Angle Variants if needed
→ Workflow B copy approval
→ Stop Slop QA
→ Quality Gate / render check when needed
```

## Mechanism Family Routing Table

| family_id | Use when the idea starts with... | Best workflow entry | Creative decision to make | Must pass |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `failure-warning` | a mistake, collapse, regret, or avoidable loss | `post-concept-flow` | what consequence makes the viewer self-audit? | account claim safety + Stop Slop QA |
| `invisible-competition` | effort, rivals, unseen standards, private discipline | `angle-variants` | who is doing the unseen work while the viewer rests? | account voice + no empty shame |
| `self-sabotage-diagnosis` | a hidden behavior causing underperformance | `post-concept-flow` | what does the viewer think the problem is, and what is it really? | science/source safety for athlete-max |
| `red-flag-fix` | visible mistakes, signals, red flags, quick corrections | `hook-idea-extraction` | what is the red flag, what does it signal, what is the micro-fix? | avoid macho/generic wording |
| `ranked-review` | too many methods, drills, choices, routines, or habits | `angle-variants` | what ranking logic proves the winner? | real ranking logic, not decorative scoring |
| `challenge-standard` | a hard benchmark or elite standard | `angle-variants` | what hidden standard separates elite from average? | no copied celebrity challenge wording |
| `surprising-proof-stat` | a shocking stat, streak, record, or anomaly | `post-concept-flow` | what question does the number force the viewer to ask? | source verification |
| `private-habit-stack` | rituals, routines, quiet edge, preparation windows | `post-concept-flow` | what private behavior compounds into public proof? | specificity; no wellness fluff |
| `hyperbolic-transformation` | dramatic before/after, fast change, extreme result | `angle-variants` | how can the claim be made tangible without false promises? | soften time-window claims |
| `roi-ranking` | highest-return habits or priorities | `post-concept-flow` | what one desire does every ranked item serve? | biological ROI ranking rules |

## Account Fit Router

### athlete-max

Use Creative DNA when the mechanism can become:

- mechanism-first performance explanation
- biological or behavioral cause → performance cost
- ranked protocol or red-flag diagnostic
- clean, clinical, source-aware carousel

Reject or reshape when the mechanism depends on:

- motivational hype
- macho shame
- unverifiable neuroscience
- random tips without one performance desire

### athlete-stories

Use Creative DNA when the mechanism can become:

- fact-led athlete story
- pressure or origin arc
- private mental/routine standard
- surprising stat or hidden method behind a legend

Reject or reshape when the mechanism depends on:

- generic advice
- clinical explanation without story
- forced product placement
- unsupported performance guarantees

## Workflow Gate Meaning

- `hook-idea-extraction`: use when the source post itself needs abstraction into a reusable hook/pattern.
- `post-concept-flow`: use when we have a broad idea, audience pain, or account concept but not final copy.
- `angle-variants`: use when multiple mechanism families could fit and we need three distinct angle options.
- `workflow-b-new-post`: use only after the concept/angle/copy direction is locked enough to draft final slide-by-slide copy.
- `stop-slop`: use before presenting final copy to remove generic AI writing and weak phrasing.
- `account-quality-gate`: use when the mechanism creates a new account pattern or risks drifting from approved writing/design rules.

## Retrieval Rule

When ideating, search by mechanism family first, not niche.

Bad:

```text
Find soccer examples.
```

Better:

```text
Find invisible-competition or private-habit-stack examples that can become a soccer confidence post.
```
