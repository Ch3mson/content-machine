## How To Answer “I Need Ideas For Account X About Y”

1. Identify the account.
2. Read that account profile in `references/creative-dna/account-profiles/`.
3. Identify the audience tension in the request:
   - fear of falling behind
   - hidden mistake
   - too many choices
   - pressure/composure
   - private preparation
   - dramatic transformation
   - surprising proof/stat
4. Select 2-4 mechanism families from the fit matrix.
5. Pull reference posts from `cross-niche-principle-map.md` and the extraction files.
6. Generate concept seeds, not final copy.
7. Route to the correct workflow:
   - broad idea → `post-concept-flow`
   - multiple angles needed → `angle-variants`
   - source post needs abstraction → `hook-idea-extraction`
   - locked copy creation → Workflow B
   - final cleanup → Stop Slop QA
   - new account pattern or design risk → quality gate
