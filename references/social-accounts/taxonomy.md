# Social Account Hook Taxonomy

These labels are used in `post-index.md`. They are intentionally reusable across
niches, so one soccer hook can be adapted to football, study, discipline, or a
personal account without copying the source wording.

## Extraction Priority

Prioritize hook-card extraction when a post has:

- A portable hook that can work outside its original niche.
- A clear emotional lever.
- A reusable flow structure, not just a good image.
- Low risk of copying unique wording or unverifiable claims.
