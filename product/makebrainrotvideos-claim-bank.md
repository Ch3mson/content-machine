## How To Use

Before writing copy that mentions the product, earnings, or results, check this
file. Use approved claims freely. Flag caution claims for review. Never use
blocked claims.

## Caution Claims

Use with care. Qualify or soften when possible.

| Claim | Risk | How to use safely |
| --- | --- | --- |
| $10,000+ per month potential | Income claim; regulatory and platform risk | Always qualify: "top creators report," never guarantee |
| Millions of views | Outcome varies wildly | Use as aspiration, not promise; reference platform examples |
| Go viral | Implies guaranteed outcome | Frame as goal or possibility, not certainty |
| Passive income | FTC scrutiny on income claims | Pair with effort framing: "after you put in the work" |
| Replace your job | Extreme income implication | Avoid unless quoting a verified creator story |

## Blocked Claims

Never use these in any content.

| Claim | Why it is blocked |
| --- | --- |
| Guaranteed income or earnings | FTC violation risk; no outcome can be guaranteed |
| No effort or work required | Misleading; content still requires strategy and iteration |
| Videos are indistinguishable from human-made | Misleading quality claim |
| You will go viral | Outcome guarantee |
| Specific earnings without verification | Fabricated social proof |
| Get rich quick framing | Platform policy violation; brand damage |
| Fake testimonials or screenshots | Legal liability; trust destruction |
