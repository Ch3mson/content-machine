## Core Product Flow

1. Creator enters a prompt or chooses from hundreds of viral prompt templates.
2. AI generates multiple video variations instantly.
3. Creator downloads and posts to TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube.

Additional feature: AI Content Creator (recently launched) for more advanced
content generation workflows.
